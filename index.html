About Kuppam

Location

Located 115.8 kilometers south-east of Bangalore and 243 kilometers west of Chennai, Kuppam is a vibrant city in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh.

Demographics

Population: 21,963 (2011 census)
Languages: Telugu (53.73%), Tamil (35.14%), Kannada (3.51%)

Economy

Known for its granite industry, educational institutions, and agricultural produce. Home to the famous 'Kuppam Green' granite.

Why Visit Kuppam?

Educational Hub

Home to prestigious institutions including Dravidian University, P.E.S. Medical College, Kuppam Engineering College, and Agastya International Foundation's 172-acre Campus Creativity Lab.
